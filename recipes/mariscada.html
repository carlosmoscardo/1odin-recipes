Seafood Recipe

Description

Seafood platter is a dish that brings together a variety of fresh seafood, usually cooked on the grill, steamed, or in a light sauce to enhance its natural flavor. It may include lobster, shrimp, mussels, clams, razor clams, and other seafood, accompanied by lemon, garlic, parsley, and olive oil.

Served hot and with a generous presentation, the seafood platter is an ideal choice for seafood lovers, offering a combination of intense textures and flavors. It is often enjoyed with crusty bread and a good white wine, highlighting the freshness and quality of the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Lobster
  • Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Clams
  • Razor clams
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • White wine

Steps to follow

  1. Wash the seafood well, removing any impurities and sand from the mussels, clams, and razor clams.
  2. Heat a large griddle or pan with a little olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the lobster and shrimp first, searing them on both sides until they take on color. Set aside.
  4. Add the mussels, clams, and razor clams, cover, and cook until they open.
  5. Prepare a paste by mixing chopped garlic, parsley, and lemon juice with a little olive oil.
  6. Pour the paste over the seafood, season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for one more minute.
  7. Serve hot, accompanied by crusty bread and a glass of white wine.
